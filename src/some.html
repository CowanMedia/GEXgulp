Dosimetry Systems

Complete solutions for B3, FWT, CTA, PMMA and Alanine

GEX offers complete dosimetry solutions for radiation processing applications involving gamma, electron beam and X-ray sources of ionizing radiation, with a dose range capability of <10 Gy to 300 kGy.

Calibration Services

Dosimeter Batch Calibration Services | GEX International Standards Laboratory

We offer formalized, complete turnkey dosimeter batch calibration services for a wide range of applications. GEX is an international standards laboratory accredited by NVLAP to ISO/IEC 17025:2005 for calibrations and dose measurements for ionizing radiation for high-dose dosimetry in the range 0.25 kGy to 100 kGy.
